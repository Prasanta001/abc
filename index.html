❝ Redefining Mentor-Student Relation❞

GIMT Student Mentor System has tons of advantages. The Mentors guide the students and addressing their issues and complaints through this system. Read about the various features of this website below.


Whether it is about installing a hardware in your department laboratory or complaints regarding the bus service, easily express your concerns through this web-based system.

Are you unsure about when the next class test will be held, or are you thinking of requesting permission for a leave of absence? Just Log in and ask your mentor through the system.

Whether it is 7 AM or 7 PM, register your complaints and queries to your concerned Mentor anytime and anywhere using the system without having to meet the Mentor personally.

Keep yourself updated about your attendance status, academic progress and other achievements and events within your department or college by logging in the system.
